(Welcome to this game!
There are three possible levels to choose from:
Level 1 is guessing a number between 1-25. Level 2 is guessing a number from 1-50 and level 3 is from 1-100.
The higher the level and less guesses it takes you to guess the correct number, the higher your score will be!
After each guess, you will be told whether your guess is greater or less than the correct number.
Good luck!!
